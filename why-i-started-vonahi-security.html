Why I Started Vonahi Security

Building My Vision

At every single penetration testing consultancy I worked for, there was a common problem that I noticed. Most of the services offered by these companies relied purely on a penetration tester's expertise.

This manual-focused approach has many disadvantages, including: high rates due to the manual labor, high turnover, inconsistent quality, limited scalability, and long turnaround times. And because of the high costs and long turnaround times, most businesses end up reducing the frequency of security assessments, resulting in less secure businesses.

I saw a clear gap in the market: businesses needed more frequent, more affordable, and more efficient security testing. This realization sparked the idea for Vonahi Security and our automated penetration testing platform.

So I started Vonahi Security to take on the challenge of improving penetration testing by leveraging automation. We'd have a team of experts who focused on reviewing automated results and providing customized recommendations, rather than spending all of their time doing manual work that could be automated. This would mean we could offer higher quality, more affordable, and faster penetration tests, which would mean safer businesses.

This idea became vPenTest – our flagship product and the industry's first automated network penetration testing platform. With vPenTest, we've been able to revolutionize how businesses approach security testing, especially for mid-market organizations that previously couldn't afford frequent testing.

In 2023, Vonahi Security was acquired by Kaseya, which has allowed us to bring our innovative approach to cybersecurity to an even wider audience. The journey from seeing a problem in the industry to creating a solution that ultimately got acquired has been incredibly rewarding. And it all started with the simple idea that security testing should be more accessible and efficient for everyone.
