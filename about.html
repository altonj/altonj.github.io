Professional Profile

The Human Behind the Code.

From script kiddie in New Orleans to selling a cybersecurity company to Kaseya.

What I Do

I live at the intersection of coding, cybersecurity, and AI. I'm obsessed with building automated tools that most people think are impossible to build.

I thrive on solving high-stakes technical problems by writing code that actually works where others give up. Whether it's securing an infrastructure or optimizing a complex workflow, I'm all about creative engineering that delivers real, measurable impact.

What I Enjoy

When I'm not building software or hacking, I'm usually deep in a hands-on technical project. I'm a car enthusiast who enjoys racing and automotive photography, and I've recently been spending more time with 3D printing and robotics.

I also enjoy playing Billiards as often as possible and always looking for the next thing to build or "figure out."
