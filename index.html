About me

Alton Johnson is the Founder and Principal Security Consultant of Vonahi Security. With over a decade of hacking experience, he’s known for the dozens of tools he developed and published on GitHub, Metasploit Framework, and Kali Linux. As a visionary business leader with over 12 industry-related certifications, he’s introducing automation to cybersecurity, making it more efficient for businesses in all verticals to scale and assess their risks in today’s digital age.


Aside from cybersecurity, Alton is also a car enthusiast where he modifies and races his car on the tracks. Alton also enjoys photography, especially automotive photography, as well as playing Billiards as often as possible.
